Properties of Gypsum pastes

Settings

The time taken from mixing gypsum and water to when the material reaches its required hardness is called the setting time. For pure gypsum paste, this time is typically around 30 minutes. Anhydrous gypsum or impure pastes may have varying setting times. This property is crucial for shaping the material effectively. In India, it plays a key role in ensuring the desired texture, finish, and durability in gypsum plastering.

Plasticity

Plasticity refers to how easily plaster or mortar can be shaped and spread. For proper application, it is essential that the mixture is neither too sticky nor too stiff. In India, the plasticity of gypsum plaster is primarily influenced by the amount of water in the mix. Additionally, the sand content can also impact its plasticity, affecting how easily the plaster can be applied and manipulated during the plastering process.

Strength

The compressive strength of gypsum plaster and mortars is typically three or more times greater than their tensile strength. Gypsum plaster exhibits high compressive strength, making it ideal for construction and building projects. This characteristic is particularly valuable in gypsum plastering applications in India, where durability and strength are essential for long-lasting results.

ADVANTAGES OF GYPSUM PLASTERING

Quick Setting

Gypsum plaster sets rapidly, cutting down the time needed for each application layer. This makes it an excellent choice for projects demanding quick completion and efficiency.

Smooth Finish

Gypsum plaster provides a smooth, refined finish that enhances the look of walls and ceilings. It delivers a sleek, high-quality surface thatâ€™s ready for painting or decorating.

Lightweight Material

Gypsum plaster is significantly lighter than traditional cement plaster, reducing the overall structural load on buildings and making it easier to handle during application.

Durability

Once hardened, gypsum plaster provides a resilient and long-lasting surface. It resists cracking and offers superior adhesion, making it ideal for applying paints and finishes.

Sound Insulation

Gypsum plaster helps minimize sound transmission between rooms, contributing to quieter and more peaceful indoor spaces.

Thermal Insulation

Gypsum plaster offers excellent thermal insulation, maintaining consistent indoor temperatures. This contributes to enhanced energy efficiency by reducing the need for excessive heating or cooling.

WHAT SERVICE WE DO.

1. Interior Gypsum Plastering.

Interior gypsum plastering involves applying a smooth layer of gypsum-based plaster to internal walls and ceilings. It offers a sleek, paint-ready surface with excellent finish quality. This method is fast, dust-free, and eliminates the need for traditional curing. It enhances the aesthetics of interior spaces while providing thermal and sound insulation. Ideal for homes, offices, and commercial buildings, it ensures long-lasting durability.

2. Exterior Gypsum Plastering.

Exterior gypsum plastering is the application of specially formulated gypsum plaster on outer walls to protect and enhance building facades. It offers a smooth, weather-resistant surface that can withstand harsh environmental conditions. The plaster is reinforced with additives to improve durability and reduce moisture absorption. This method ensures faster application and reduced maintenance compared to traditional cement plasters. Ideal for modern constructions, it provides both aesthetic appeal and long-term performance.
