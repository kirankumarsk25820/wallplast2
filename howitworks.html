Surface Cleaning & Pre-Treatment

Before we begin plastering, the wall or ceiling surface is thoroughly cleaned. We remove all dust, grease, and loose materials to ensure the gypsum bonds effectively. If the base is highly porous or dry, we may lightly dampen it to reduce suction and enhance adhesion. This crucial step ensures a strong foundation for the plaster to hold on to, preventing future issues like peeling or cracks.

Accurate Mixing of Gypsum Plaster

Our team prepares the gypsum mix by combining the right ratio of powder to clean water. The result is a smooth, creamy, and lump-free paste ready for application. We use high-quality mixers to maintain consistency. Some of our products come pre-mixed, reducing on-site preparation time. This phase is key to ensuring the plaster has the ideal working time, setting speed, and finish quality.

Professional Plaster Application

The mixed plaster is applied to the surface using hand tools or spray machines, depending on project size and complexity. Our applicators use skilled techniques to ensure uniform coverage, correct thickness, and no air pockets. The first layer is carefully leveled, and if needed, additional layers are added to achieve the desired finish. This ensures durability and a seamless appearance across the entire surface.

Surface Levelling & Smoothing

After application, the surface is leveled using aluminum straight edges and smoothed with steel trowels. We focus on aligning the plaster properly to avoid uneven areas, bumps, or waves. For high-end finishes, a second or skim coat may be applied to enhance smoothness, especially for decorative or paint-ready surfaces. This step gives your walls a sleek, professional-grade finish that stands out.

Quick Setting & Natural Drying

Gypsum plaster sets much faster than cement plasterâ€”typically within 30 to 45 minutes. Once the plaster is set, it begins to harden and cure naturally without requiring water curing. The quick drying time speeds up the construction process and reduces labor. Depending on ambient conditions and thickness, full drying may take a few hours to a day.

Final Touches & Ready for Painting

Once fully dry, the plastered surface is checked for any minor imperfections. We perform a light sanding or polishing if needed. At this stage, the surface is completely ready for paint or wallpaperâ€”no additional putty or primers are required. The result is a bright, clean, and durable finish that enhances the beauty and longevity of your interior or exterior walls.
